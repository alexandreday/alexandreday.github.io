About Me

Thanks for visiting my website ! I am a PhD candidate at Boston University doing research with Pankaj Mehta. I am working on and thinking about theoretical aspects of machine learning and applying a wide range of ML methods to solve problems from physics and biology. For an introduction to machine learning, check out our introductory review to ML supplemented with many useful Python Jupyter notebooks. I'm always open to an impromptu chat about ML, physics and science in general! You can find my full CV here.
